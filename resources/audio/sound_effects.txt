=== inc_dec_pan_test ===

    set_gain        0x7f

    set_pan         0

    play_note       d4  no_keyoff  16

    start_loop
        inc_pan         2
        rest            1
    end_loop        68

    rest 16

    start_loop
        dec_pan         2

    end_loop        68
